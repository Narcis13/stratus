## Operating notes

- **Scheduling:** load these into the stratus calendar as `pending` rows at the exact times above (already jittered). The ⚠️ receipt posts should sit as `draft` until the day's real numbers go in — never let the publisher ship a placeholder.
- **The scoreboard posts are the spine.** Days 1, 4, 7, 11, 14 form a serial narrative; followers gained mid-run will scroll back and read it as a story. Everything else is engine.
- **Comment discipline beats post quality.** His formula for exactly this phase (0→1k): "a few posts a day + 50–100 replies, ~2 hrs/day." Your 10–20 replies/day doctrine is the floor; the two Friday connectors and the Day 3 game are commitments to 60+ replies on the day.
- **If a post dies** (<5 comments in 2h), don't delete — reply to it yourself with a sharper follow-up question. If a post flies, quote-reup it ~24h later (`POST /x/posts/reup`).
- **Week 3 exists.** Whatever the number on Day 14, the final post promises "round two" — draft the next 14 days from whichever of these 28 earned the most comments, remixing your own winners this time.
